Kitchen Helper

For part of my capstone project, I designed the concept and interface for a futuristic cooking app named Kitchen Helper. Implemented with augmented reality contact lenses, Kitchen Helper provides a variety of recipes and the means to execute them. It allows users to find recipes that suit the ingredients of their fridge, order custom-portioned ingredients to their door, and get tips throughout the cooking process.

Who am I?

My journey in design began as a 13 year old on a website called Neopets. First infatuated with designing my profile, I then became obsessed with HTML and web design. I’ve since expanded my obsession with creativity to a variety of mediums including visual design and animation.

By day, I’m committed to creating design and media with the purpose of helping businesses express themselves and grow. I love trying new creative mediums and applying them to a client’s endeavors.

By night, I’m a learner and reader, a ukulele enthusiast, a teller of Dad Jokes™, and an (unfortunately) bad plant parent. I also volunteer with AIGA Boston—catch me helping out at their events!

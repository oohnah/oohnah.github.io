Gravity Girl

Gravity Girl is a hero that I created for my character design class. She has the power to change her gravitational pull to attract and repel objects around her. She is part of a larger universe that I had already created, dubbed the Pocket Girl Universe after my first hero character, Pocket Girl. The universe consists of heroes who have powers based on pseudo science, and you can see more work relating to Pocket Girl and Gravity Girl HERE.

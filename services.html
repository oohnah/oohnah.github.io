Let's dive right in!

I take a custom, collaborative approach to each project. After hearing your thoughts, I like to take a look at your business goals and how creative can help reach them.

Branding

If you're looking to update your brand identity, I'll work with you to come up with a look and feel for connecting your mission, product, or services to your audience. Brand projects can range from a logo, typogrphy, and color palette, to a full system that includes custom illustration and motion guidelines.

Web Design

If you're looking to update or redo you website, I'm well versed both in creating Figma mockups as well as working with CMS to develop the full site. Currently I offer websites in Squarespace, WordPress, and Webflow, but I consider myself quick at learning new softwares and am interested in working in other programs like Wix and Framer.

Websites can be bundled with branding if you want to get everything set up or revamped at once!

Motion, Social, and Other projects

I have experience with social campaigns, motion graphics, annual reports, ebooks, tradeshow banners, and other types of design collateral to support your brand.

Willow Branding & Landing Page

An optimistic brand for the future of healthcare

Building a brand

Role: Art Director & Designer

Willow connects patients and providers by facilitating
easy communication and transfer of patient information.
To help portray Willow as a friendly but modern medical app,
I created a brand that utilizes bright colors, clean, geometric
type, and a pattern that visualizes the transfer of patient data.

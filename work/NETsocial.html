Netrality Social Media Template

Design System for Social

A Cohesive Design System for Social Media

Role: Designer
Design Direction: Natalie Ferro

Our client asked for social media templates for Twitter and LinkedIn. We started by taking an audit of the types of content that they typically post. Then we designed a cohesive system that allowed the client to use different photos and illustrations that could keep posts unique while remaining cohesive. The consistency in type and layout give the templates a common thread that create cohesion.

Who am I?

My journey in design began as a 13 year old on a website called Neopets. First infatuated with designing my profile, I then became obsessed with HTML and web design. I’ve since expanded my obsession with creativity to a variety of mediums including visual design and animation. This website is still coded from scratch!

By day, I’m committed to creating design and media to help clients connect with their audiences and grow. I'm particularly interested in working with clients who are mission driven, are non-profits, or are working to help solve climate change.

By night, I’m a learner and reader, a ukulele enthusiast, a teller of Dad Jokes™, and an (unfortunately) bad plant parent. I also volunteer with a climate org called Sunrise, a turtle rescue by Earthlings NYC, and an indie music organization called Keepsake House.
